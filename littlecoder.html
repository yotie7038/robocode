Little Coder

The Little Coder course is designed to introduce young minds to the exciting world of programming through interactive and playful learning. Using visual coding platforms like Scratch and Blockly, kids develop fundamental computational thinking skills while creating animations, games, and simple projects. This hands-on approach nurtures creativity and problem-solving abilities, making coding fun and engaging for beginners.

In addition to coding logic, the course focuses on essential design principles, helping kids understand user-friendly interfaces and basic storytelling elements in their projects. Through structured lessons and guided challenges, students build confidence in coding while developing a strong foundation for more advanced programming concepts in the future.
